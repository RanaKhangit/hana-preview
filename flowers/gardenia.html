The flower of the quietest truths

The Power of the FlowerGardenia is a flower for inner peace and quiet devotion.

Greek myth made the gardenia sacred to the god of dreams, a white bloom whose scent was thought to carry the heart somewhere gentler. In the HANA garden it is the flower of inner peace and the truths kept closest.

The meaning of Gardenia

In ancient Greek mythology, gardenia was sacred to Morpheus, god of dreams—called 'Moon tears' or 'Moon fruit' in lore, believed to transport the soul to Elysian paradise through its aroma. Chinese tradition used gardenia in love potions, honouring its delicate beauty as a vehicle for unspoken affection. Victorian flower language assigned gardenia as a language of secret love—a bloom exchanged between lovers to convey hidden devotion. The gentle scent has long been associated with peace, self-awareness, and spiritual protection.

A flower essence is not an aromatic oil; Gardenia is present here for its lore, not its fragrance.
