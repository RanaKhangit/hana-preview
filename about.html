THE FOUNDER’S APOTHECARY

A flower can be a quiet companion

HANA began in a small, light-filled room of pressed flowers and glass — the world of Aliya, founder and botanical storyteller, who has spent years listening to what flowers mean to us when we simply let them keep us company.

Her belief is gentle and firm: a flower offers a moment of reflection, presence and symbolic meaning. It meets a feeling with its own character — warmth, stillness, brightness, calm — and reminds us, softly, of something we already hold. That is the whole of HANA: feeling, ritual and old apothecary lore, composed into a small object made for one person.

Every blend is arranged by hand, balanced by season and by the quiet proportions the old apothecaries knew. Nothing is mass-made. Nothing is presented as a health claim — HANA speaks through ritual, symbolism and botanical story. Everything is meant to be beautiful, and yours.

— Aliya, founder of HANA
